ABOUT THE PLATFORM

ExciteUs is a brand new platform launched in Leuven to help the youth to achieve the perfect night out. In the app the users can find data, opening and closing times, a list of events, promo’s and bars. With one click the users can log in to facebook or google and then they can see a detailed list of all the bars and events. When they are interested in a specific event or bar they can click on that said event and get more detailed info. On top of that, there is the possibility to add photo’s to a specific bar or event so the users who aren’t there at the moment can enjoy the party and even look the party/bar up to see if there is a nice atmosphere. In short, the ideal guide for a night out.
